How it works

Built for the way
collectors actually operate.

01

Connect once, sync forever

Connect your eBay account via OAuth. Ripster pulls your complete transaction history — every sale, every purchase, every fee — and keeps it updated automatically. No CSV files. No manual entry.

02

Every card gets an ID

Ripster's Card ID system links your purchase to its eventual sale — across any platform combination. Raw buy on PayPal, graded sale on eBay. The pairing is automatic. True per-card ROI, finally.

03

Tax output, not guesswork

Schedule D export. 1099-K reconciliation. Grading fee basis additions. Hobby vs. dealer classification tracking. The IRS complexity of collecting, handled automatically. Export to your CPA in one click.

Tax tools

The 28% rate
is real. So is
your exposure.

Collectibles are taxed differently than stocks. Ripster is the only tracker built specifically for this complexity.

📋

Schedule D / Form 8949 export

One-click export formatted exactly for your CPA — proceeds, cost basis, acquisition date, sale date, holding period, and short/long-term classification per card.

⚡

1099-K reconciliation

Your 1099-K reports gross sales — not your actual net gain. Ripster documents the difference automatically, including platform fees, shipping, and cost of goods.

⏱

Holding period optimizer

Alerts you when cards are approaching the one-year long-term threshold. For collectors in the 37% bracket, crossing it saves 9+ points on federal tax alone.
